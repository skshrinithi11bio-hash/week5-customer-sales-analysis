 Week 5-Customer Sales Analysis Report 
1. Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer sales data to understand purchasing patterns, revenue distribution, and the impact of customer churn. Using Python and pandas, sales data was merged with churn data to extract meaningful business insights.

2. Objective

i)Analyze customer sales performance

ii)Identify revenue contribution based on churn status

iii)Understand regional and product-wise sales trends

iv)Provide insights for improving customer retention

3. Datasets Used

   1.sales_data.csv – contains product, quantity, price, region, and customer information

   2.customer_churn.csv – contains churn status of customers

4. Tools & Technologies

Python,Google Colab,Pandas,Matplotlib

5. Data Processing Steps

Mounted Google Drive and loaded CSV files,Handled missing values,Created a revenue column,Merged sales and churn datasets,Performed aggregations and pivot table analysis,Visualized key metrics using charts

6. Key Analysis Performed

Total revenue calculation

Average order value computation

Revenue grouped by churn status

Regional revenue comparison

Product-wise revenue distribution

7. Visualizations

Bar chart showing revenue by churn status

Bar chart showing regional sales distribution

Pie chart showing product-wise revenue contribution

8. Insights & Recommendations

Non-churned customers contribute the majority of revenue

Certain regions generate higher sales, indicating strong market presence

A small group of products drives most revenue

Customer churn results in potential revenue loss; retention strategies are recommended

9. Conclusion

This analysis demonstrates how customer sales and churn data can be combined to gain actionable business insights. The project successfully applies data manipulation, aggregation, merging, and visualization techniques using Python.
